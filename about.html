Intro

As a newbie developer, I'm interested in interwining my journey towards zero waste/minimalism with the tech world. I believe that technology has the ability to improve the way we live, while also helping us move away from the negatives of the consumeristic and industrialized effects on society. This website is a record and an expression of my journey. I hope to continue to evolve and acquire more skills to improve my life and others around me.

Zero Waste Minimalist Tenets

Zero waste and mininamlism I believe are both concepts that complement each other. I believe that technology and the sharing economy has improved modern living in a way we can think more consciously about what we consume and how much of it we consume.

Refuse > Reduce > Reuse > Rot > Recycle > Redesign

Recycling and Redesigning are respectively less desirable and requires large societal changes. Whereas at an individual level we can most likely Refuse, Reduce, Reuse and Rot. All of which can produce less carbon footpint and eliminate the unnecessary excess from our lives.
