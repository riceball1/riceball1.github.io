About Dana Ng

Hello, my name is Dana Ng, and I'm based in San Francisco Bay Area. I'm a web developer who loves to build clean, efficient web applications focusing on the full stack. Currently I'm coding in JavaScript with experience in Node.js, MongoDB, React and Redux.

Besides learning more about programming, I'm also an aspiring polyglot, currently learning Mandarin, Turkish and French, with fluency in English and limited working proficiency in Cantonese. I like staying active outdoors by traveling, hiking and playing soccer. In addition, I'm a foodie and amateur chef. I hope to combine my interests in living a minimalist and sustainable lifestyle with my work. Feel free to connect with me.
