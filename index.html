less is more

Since I adopted minimalism I have found a true purpose in my life. Every day I find ways to improve my carbon footprint on this planet and to inspire others to do so. As a noob developer, I'm interested in interwining my journey towards zero waste with the tech world. I believe that technology has the ability to improve the way we live, while also helping us move away from the negatives of the consumeristic and industrialized effects on society. This website is a record and an expression of my journey. I hope to continue to evolve and acquire more skills to improve my life and others around me.
